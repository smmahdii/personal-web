About Me

I am a passionate educator and graphic designer with a deep commitment to fostering creativity and knowledge. My journey combines the technical world of Computer Science & Technology with the artistic realm of graphic design.

Currently teaching Diploma in Computer Science & Technology subjects at Papel Edu Care and affiliated with ACTC, I believe in creating engaging learning experiences that blend technical knowledge with creative expression.

When I'm not teaching or designing, I enjoy reading works by Humayun Ahmed and exploring new ways to express ideas through writing and visual media. My philosophy centers on the belief that education and creativity are powerful tools for personal growth and societal development.

My Philosophy

"Education is not just about transferring knowledge; it's about inspiring curiosity and nurturing creativity. Every student has unique potential waiting to be unlocked through the right blend of technical skills and creative expression."
